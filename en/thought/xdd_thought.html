Socialist Thought with Chinese Characteristics in the New Era

Socialism with Chinese characteristics has entered a new era, which is a new historical orientation for China's development.
Socialism with Chinese characteristics in the new era of Jinping embodies the latest achievements of contemporary Chinese Marxism, and is the action program and scientific theoretical guidance for the development of the cause of the Party and the state for a considerable period of time at present and in the future.
Deeply implementing Xi Jinping's socialist thought with Chinese characteristics in the new era will surely contribute to building a well-off society in an all-round way.
To seize the new victory of socialist modernization has gathered great and immense strength.

Initial Heart and Mission, the Fundamental Motivation to Encourage Progress

Xi Jinping's socialist thought with Chinese characteristics in the new era deeply
contains the initial intention and mission of the Communists of China.

一、Historical status

The report of the Nineteenth National Congress pointed out that "after long-term efforts, socialism with Chinese characteristics has entered a new era, which is a new historical orientation for China's development".

Adhering to and developing socialism with Chinese characteristics is a distinct theme of contemporary China. The report of the Nineteenth National Congress pointed out that "socialism with Chinese characteristics is the theme of all the theory and practice of the Party since the reform and opening up, and the fundamental achievement that the Party and the people have made through untold hardships and great costs".

二、Bright theme

三、Goal of struggle

The Chinese Dream is an important idea put forward by the Party Central Committee with Comrade Xi Jinping as its core, and it is the goal of China's development and progress. The report of the Nineteenth National Congress has made a new strategic arrangement for the "two goals for a hundred years".
On the basis of building a well-off society in an all-round way, we should take two steps to build a strong socialist modernization country with prosperity, democracy, civilization, harmony and beauty in the middle of this century.

The main contradiction of our society in the new era is the contradiction between the people's growing need for a better life and the unbalanced and insufficient development. In order to adapt to the new characteristics of this contradiction, we must firmly establish and consciously practice the new development ideas of innovation, coordinated development, green development, open development and shared development.

四、Development Concept

五、Development Layout

The "five in one" of economic construction, political construction, cultural construction, social construction and ecological civilization construction is the overall layout of socialism with Chinese characteristics. Building a well-off society in an all-round way, deepening reform in an all-round way, administering the country according to law and strictly administering the Party in an all-round way are the strategic layout of socialism with Chinese characteristics.

六、Development Motivation

Reform is an important driving force for social development. Deepening reform in an all-round way is an important measure to build a well-off society in an all-round way and to realize the strategic goal of the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation.

Ruling the country according to law in an all-round way will create a good environment for the rule of law and provide a strong guarantee for building a well-off society in an all-round way and realizing the new journey of the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation. We should constantly improve the legal and moral qualities of the whole nation, and adhere to the complementarity of governing the country by virtue and by law

七、Development Guarantee

八、Safety and security

The report of the Nineteenth Congress of the CPC pointed out that the goal of strengthening the Party's army in the new era should be "to build a people's army under the command of the Party, capable of winning battles and with excellent style of work, and to build the people's army into a world-class army". The strategy of strengthening the army in the new era is to persist in building up the army politically, reforming and strengthening the army, developing the army by science and technology, and administering the army according to law.

九、external environment

The report of the Nineteenth National Congress pointed out that "it is clear that the diplomacy of major powers with Chinese characteristics should promote the construction of new international relations and the construction of a community of human destiny". China should make further positive contributions to the lofty cause of promoting human peace, development and progress.

The main contradiction of our society in the new era is the contradiction between the people's growing need for a better life and the unbalanced and insufficient development. In order to adapt to the new characteristics of this contradiction, we must firmly establish and consciously practice the new development ideas of innovation, coordinated development, green development, open development and shared development.

十、Political assurance

十一、Methodology of the world outlook on governing the country and Politics

We should adhere to "emancipating the mind, seeking truth from facts, advancing with the times, seeking truth and pragmatism, adhering to dialectical materialism and historical materialism". We should adhere to scientific thinking methods, improve strategic thinking, historical thinking, dialectical thinking, systematic thinking, innovative thinking, legal thinking and bottom line thinking ability. We should also adhere to Marxist philosophy. The innovative achievements of learning are regarded as the methodology of world outlook on governing the country and politics.

十二、Values of Governance

To strive for the happiness of the Chinese people, for the revival of the Chinese nation and for the cause of human progress. We should adhere to the people-centered development concept and constantly promote economic and social development and the all-round development of people themselves. In the final analysis, the Chinese dream is the people's happy dream.
