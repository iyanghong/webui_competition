  • New Democratic Revolution

    Starting from China's historical and social conditions, this paper makes a profound study of the characteristics and laws of the Chinese revolution, develops Marxist-Leninist thought on the leadership of the proletariat in the Democratic revolution, and establishes a proletarian-led, worker-peasant alliance-based, popular opposition to imperialism, feudalism and The theory of the new democratic revolution of bureaucratic capitalism.

    In practice, the Communist Party of China has created a revolutionary road in which the countryside surrounds the city and finally captures the national victory. "The united front and armed struggle are the two basic weapons to defeat the enemy", and the Party's own construction is the "three magic weapons" of the revolution.

  • Socialist Revolution and Socialist Construction

    According to the economic conditions for the transition to socialism created by the victory of the New Democratic Revolution, we adopted the policy of simultaneous socialist industrialization and socialist transformation, and carried out the concrete policy of gradually transforming the private ownership of means of production, thus solving theoretically and practically the problem of establishing a society in a large country with backward economy and culture, such as China. The difficult task of the socialist system.

    The theory of people's democratic dictatorship put forward by Mao Zedong must strictly distinguish and correctly deal with the contradictions between the enemy and ourselves and the contradictions among the people, correctly handle the relationship between heavy industry and agriculture and light industry on the basis of agriculture, attach great importance to the development of agriculture and light industry, and walk out a road of industrialization in China that suits our national conditions. The theory of road is the basic guideline guiding our socialist construction.

  • Revolutionary Army Construction and Military Strategy

    Mao Zedong systematically solved the problem of how to build a revolutionary army with peasants as its main component into a new type of people's army with a proletarian nature, strict discipline and close ties with the people.

  • Policies and Strategies

    Mao Zedong brilliantly demonstrated that policies and strategies are the life of the Party and the starting point and destination of all practical actions of the revolutionary party. We must formulate the Party's policies according to the political situation, class relations and actual situations and their changes, and combine principle with flexibility.

  • Ideological and political work and cultural work

    Ideological and political work is the lifeline of economic work and all other work, and the policy of political and economic unity, political and technological unity, red and specialized should be implemented; on the development of national, scientific and popular culture, the policy of letting a hundred flowers blossom, weeding out the old and bringing forth the new, making the past serve the present and making foreign things serve China; and on knowledge. Intellectuals play an important role in revolution and construction. Intellectuals should combine with workers and peasants, etc.

  • Party building

    He pointed out that the style of combining theory with practice, closely linked with the masses of the people and self-criticism is a remarkable sign that the Communist Party of China is different from any other political party. In view of the "cruel struggle" and "merciless strike" left-leaning errors in the inner-Party struggle in history, he put forward the correct policy of "punishing the former and killing the latter, curing the sick and saving the people", emphasizing that in the inner-party struggle, we should not only clarify our thoughts but also unite our comrades. He created a rectification form of Marxist-Leninist ideological education through criticism and self-criticism throughout the Party.

World impact
China

Mao Zedong Thought came into being in China, a semi-colonial and semi-feudal society. It is an important historical phenomenon in the development of Chinese revolution and international communist movement.Under its guidance, the Chinese people have won great victories. Therefore, Mao Zedong Thought can be used for reference in the liberation struggle of oppressed nations in the world and in the cause of human progress.

European countries

In Europe, some left-wing political parties established under the guidance of Mao Zedong Thought in the 1960s and 1970s still adhere to Mao Zedong Thought, such as the Norwegian Communist Party of Workers, the German Malay-Leninist Party, the Turkish Kurdish Communist Party of Maoism, and the Greek Communist Organization.

Latin America

In Latin America, the "Revolutionary Communist Party" of Argentina, the "Communist Party of Peru-Glorious Road" of Peru, and the "Communist Party of Nepal (Maoism) of Asia and Nepal and the"Communist Party of the Philippines"of the Philippines are all active. Among them, the Communist Party of Nepal (Maoism) came to power in 1994, came to power through general elections in 2008, and transformed Nepal into a republic.
