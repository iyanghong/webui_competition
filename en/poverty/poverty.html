The Long March in My Heart"

Baini village in Shishan is a deeply impoverished village. There are 195 impoverished households in 472 households in the village. Eleven natural villages are scattered. The farthest one is 13 kilometers away from the village, and several of them are more than 10 kilometers away. When he first arrived in the village, Huang Wenxiu met a nail.

In order to gain the trust of the masses, we must treat them as relatives from the heart, urgently think about what they want, and truly integrate with them. Huang Wenxiu learned the truth by consulting his colleagues with experience in the village and the old branch books in the village. Soon she changed her working methods, and instead of asking questions in books, she took off her coat and helped clean the yard to do farm work. When the poor families were not at home, she went to the fields to help them pick sugar oranges, grow oil tea, and work at the same time, talking about the family routine; she did not speak Mandarin, but learned to speak dialect...

Huang Wenxiu often did not go home at weekends. He visited all the poor households in the village. He also drew the "distribution map of the poor households" in the village. The address, family situation and causes of poverty of each household were marked in his notebook one by one. From the beginning of accepting Huang Wenxiu, the masses loved and respected her in their hearts. Some people joked, "You're such a tough girl to get entangled with!"

Huang Wenxiu once said to his friends, "In the Long March, soldiers are not afraid of death. How can this difficulty limit my progress on the road to poverty alleviation?" "As the first secretary stationed in the village, he will never win a victory, and never take troops!"

I hope every villager has a good life.

The villagers in Zhaxigang village live scattered, and Luotuo has to walk more than ten kilometers every day for work. On the way, when I meet the villagers, I always greet Luo Tuo warmly and take her along on my own initiative. There are 88 poor households in the village, and the specific conditions of each household are different. Luo Tsuo is very clear about the 173 Poverty Alleviation Policies of the state, who needs help from the furniture body, the children going to school, the elderly seeing a doctor, the housing improvement and so on.

The Rosandandandanda family in the village is the most reassuring one in Luotuo. The house was cracked and Rosandandandanda's wife and eldest daughter were sick all the year round. Considering the specific situation of Rosandandanda's family, Lotso suggested that Rosandanda move to a centralized settlement, so that Rosandanda and his family can not only have free housing, but also have more convenient medical treatment and employment. But the Rosandanas could not give up the land they had planted for half their lives. They were always reluctant to accept Lotso's proposal for relocation and refused countless times. She did not give up easily and painstakingly contacted the tenants who were willing to contract for the land of Rosandandanda family. She took Rosandanda to the resettlement site to experience the environment here personally. With Lotso's continuous efforts, the Rosandandanda family was finally persuaded. Rosandandandanda, who relocated to the resettlement site, said happily, "It's very good and beautiful. Now I'm very happy to see it. The quality of the house is good. Anyway, everything is good. I want to thank the Party and the government."

Start with small things

The Wachang village of Gongchenyu poverty alleviation is a multi-ethnic administrative village composed of Zhuang, Miao, Han and Yi nationalities. It is located in the rocky desertification area of southern Yunnan. There are less than 1 800 people in the village, but more than one third of the poor people.

In September 2017, Gong Chenyu learned that he had spent most of his savings because of the death of his elderly family. Although the local civil affairs department had sent him relief food, his daughter's pressure to study and the cost of restoring production at home kept him frowning all day. With Gong Chenyu's help, a loving person from Beijing took the initiative to form a "help couple" with his family, offering to pay for all the study expenses his daughter needed until she graduated from university, and transferred 2,000 yuan to his family through Wechat. "One village has one village. It's really not easy to get rid of poverty in Wachang village." Gong Chenyu was no longer in such a hurry when he entered the village for more than four months.

Mobilize big households to take the lead in trying out high-efficiency varieties, contact big households to buy on their doors, and mobilize villagers to sell online... He said that poverty alleviation has a long way to go, and more confidence is needed; to help the unpaid villagers contact legal aid, invite regular teachers for the village literary and art team to carry out training, inherit the "Sun Ancient Song", and register trademarks and domain names for the local supporting national cultural industries. He said that only by doing a good job of small things can we "sing a song and get rid of poverty" with the people of the nation.

Qin Xining

"The road into the village is still a long mud road, clear and rainy. Six natural villages have not yet hardened their roads to villages, five Miao villages are seriously short of water, and some of the poor people still live a life of inseparability between human beings and livestock. This is Qin Xining's first impression of Gedang Village. On January 13, 2016, Qin Xining, a cadre of the International Cooperation Bureau of the Supreme People's Procuratorate, came to Gedang Village, Xinhua Town, Funing County, Yunnan Province, to serve as the first Secretary of poverty alleviation in the village, shouldering the important task of fighting poverty in Gedang Village. That year, when he was 29 years old, his first birthday gift was a hand-painted "battle map" of the geographical distribution of 13 natural villages in Gedang Village. Qin Xining started from visiting 88 filing and card-making households in 13 villages under the jurisdiction of Gedang Village, and went deep into the villagers'homes to have a heart-to-heart talk with them to understand the villagers' income and expenditure situation and difficulties at home.

The eyes of the masses are bright, calm and quiet. The common people will naturally realize that you are not coming to"play soy sauce"or"come and read". What's more, it's not so-called"come and gild". It's doing things for them. Qin Xining said. Only through household survey, can not fully understand the difficulties of families. Qin Xining believes that the situation should be understood in many ways. As a result, he changed his work strategy and took lessons from village cadres as soon as he had time, and talked to the villagers more about his family life, so as to carry out the precise poverty alleviation work more pertinently.

Once, Wang Yi, an old branch of the village committee, responded to Qin Xining's response that "the people's hopes for the mud road to repair the village entrance are all white." In a twinkling of an eye, Gedang Village completed the paving and opening of the oil road from 323 National Highway to 10 kilometers of the village committee's location, hardening the road into a group of 5.3 kilometers.
